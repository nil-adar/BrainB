About Us

At NODUS, we offer a virtual platform for detecting ADHD symptoms in children using modern available technology. Our platform is designed to be simplistic and easy-to-navigate, with a user-friendly focus. Our unique selling point is our innovative approach to detecting ADHD symptoms. We provide eye-tracking tests, vocal tests using a webcam and microphone, as well as reaction time tests using the mouse. Our platform is available for testing 24/7 no appointments needed!
